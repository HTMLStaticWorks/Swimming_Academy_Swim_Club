Water Safety First

Our Gold Standard Safety & Water Security

Your safety is our absolute, non-negotiable priority. We maintain state-of-the-art facilities and follow strict safety guidelines to ensure a worry-free swimming environment.

100% Certified Coaches

Every coach on our deck is fully certified in Lifeguarding, CPR/AED, and First Aid by the Red Cross, with background checks completed annually.

Pristine Water Quality

Our advanced UV disinfection and multi-media sand filters cycle the water every 4 hours, maintaining low chlorine levels safe for sensitive skin.

Active Surveillance

Dedicated, non-teaching lifeguards maintain active 10/20 scanning from high chairs at all times. Instructors never double as guards.

Emergency Response

We have fully equipped first-aid stations, automated external defibrillators (AEDs), and hold mock drills every month with our entire staff.

Proud Member of the National Drowning Prevention Alliance (NDPA) & USA Swimming Safe Sport Recognized.
