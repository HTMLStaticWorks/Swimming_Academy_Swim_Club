Our Path to Mastery

The Swimming Progression Levels

We track every student's skills through 7 structured level tiers. Here is the path your child will follow:

Level 1
Water Confidence

Submersion, breath control, and safety rules.

Level 2
Floating & Kicking

Front and back floats, flutter kicking.

Level 3
Freestyle Basics

Introduction to side breathing and freestyle arm actions.

Level 4
Freestyle & Backstroke

Consolidating strokes up to 25 meters distance.

Level 5
Breaststroke

Kick coordination and timing basics.

Level 6
Butterfly

Dolphin kicking and complete fly body mechanics.

Level 7
Competitive Readiness

Endurance testing, flip turns, and lane etiquette. Ready for club squads!
