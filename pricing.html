Curriculum Skill Breakdown

Level Title Skills Mastered to Advance
Level 1 Water Confidence Submerging mouth, nose, and eyes. Blowing bubbles, back float support, and safety exits.
Level 2 Floating & Kicking Independent front and back floats (5s), flutter kick basics, and rolling over.
Level 3 Freestyle Basics Side breathing coordination, continuous flutter kick (15m), freestyle arm sweep.
Level 4 Freestyle & Backstroke Refining backstroke arm movement, swimming 25 meters, and treading water (1 min).
Level 5 Breaststroke Breaststroke whip kick timing, coordination of glide, and basic survival floats.
Level 6 Butterfly Dolphin kick body wave coordination, synchronized arm strokes, and 50m endurance.
Level 7 Competitive Readiness Lane rules, diving starts, flip turns, and continuous 100m multi-stroke swim.
