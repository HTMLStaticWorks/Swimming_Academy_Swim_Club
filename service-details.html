Learn to Swim (Ages 3-12)

Our signature youth curriculum designed to take children from absolute water hesitancy to complete multi-stroke swimming safety and speed.

What is Covered in this Program

Our child-centric coaches break down the mechanics of the body's buoyancy and limb coordinations across various levels:

Conquering depth panic, blowing bubbles, submerging mouth and eyes safely, floating comfortably on the front and back without assistance, and basic kicking patterns.

Introducing unilateral side breathing, breaststroke arm pull-outs, freestyle strokes up to 25 meters, and backstroke flutter kick alignment.

Perfecting the dolphin kick and butterfly arm cycle, mastering flip turns at lane ends, swimming continuous 100-meter laps, and starting blocks safety.

Quick Facts

  • Duration: 45 Mins / Session
  • Class Size: Max 5 Swimmers
  • Frequency: 2 or 3 Days / Week
  • Requirement: Age 3 to 12 Years
