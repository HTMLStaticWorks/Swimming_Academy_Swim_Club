Milestone Roadmap

Our 7-Level Structured Curriculum

We monitor every child's safety and technique progress step-by-step. Our level navigator helps parents track milestones and prepare students for squad swim meets.

Levels 1-2: Water Familiarization

Focuses on breath control, independent back floating, and building water joy.

Levels 3-5: Stroke Mechanics

Introducing unilateral side breathing, breaststroke timing, and freestyle distance.

Levels 6-7: Competitive Readiness

Perfecting butterfly arm cycle, diving blocks safety, and flip turn conditioning.
